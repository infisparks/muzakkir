About Holy Crescent English High School

Founder: Mr. Mohd. Hanif Pitalwala

A visionary businessman who established H.C.E.H.S to provide quality education to children from underprivileged backgrounds.

On 1st June 1993, Mr. Mohd. Hanif Pitalwala laid the foundation of Holy Crescent English High School. Seeing the plight of children in his community who had to travel long distances and pay exorbitant fees for education, he felt the urgent need to build a school offering high-quality education at an affordable cost. It was his dream to provide the strongest base of education for children from poor localities, so they could stand on their feet and contribute to society.

He envisioned a school that would not just teach academic knowledge, but also instill values like respect for one another, discipline, and the importance of cultural activities. With this mission in mind, within just a year, he successfully founded the school.

The Birth of Abdul Hamid Pitalwala Educational Society

To support his dream, Mr. Pitalwala established the "Abdul Hamid Pitalwala Educational Society." This society consists of a team of eminent scholars, dedicated teachers, and a strong management team, all committed to providing a holistic educational experience for every child.

Our Core Values

  • Quality Education for All
  • Discipline and Respect for Others
  • Cultural Development and Activities
  • Empowerment of Underprivileged Communities

The school has grown from strength to strength, with a focus on nurturing each student's potential and ensuring they are equipped for success in their future endeavors.
